Presentation Registration

We are looking for people who are willing to do presentations, posters on the day. If you're insterested in contributing, please register from below button.

  • Title and abstract are required for both oral and poster presentations.
  • Your title and abstract will be posted on the site when adopted.
  • The application deadline is April 30th.
  • Presentations must be written in English.
  • Talks can be given in either English or Japanese.

Presentation Format

  • Oral presentation (20 min followed by 5 min for Q&A)
  • Oral presentation (15 min followed by 5 min for Q&A)
  • Poster presentation (Sized A0)
    • *The presentation format may not always be what you want.
